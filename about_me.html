Here's a little exercice, just to create a small website talking about me.

But sorry, I don't like to talk about myself, just because I think I'm not important. What is important to me is to do my best to make people happy. Only acts count and leave a mark, those who perform them only pass...

It is also important to me to leave the Earth as much as possible as I received it at my birth, which is not easy I must admit, but I try.

What does our lifespan on this billion-year-old planet represent? The equivalent of a thousandth of a second on a day? Not even. So why complicate things? My motto: life is too short to complicate it, so enjoy.

I prefer to let the mystery hang in the air and let people discover me as I am, with my qualities and my flaws. Those who know me then appreciate me at my fair value and not on base of what I could say or what people could think about me.
